Steganography securely hides messages within images and videos

Often embedded in the pixel data, ensuring that the image remains visually unchanged to the human eye. For videos, the data can be encoded in individual frames, making it virtually undetectable during playback

Hidden data remains undetectable without compromising file quality.

This method is highly effective for secure communication, as it allows sensitive information to be transmitted discreetly. Modern steganographic techniques often incorporate encryption to enhance security, making the hidden data accessible only to authorized individuals with the correct decoding key. The applications of steganography range from digital watermarking to secure data transmission in fields such as cybersecurity, digital forensics, and multimedia content protection.
